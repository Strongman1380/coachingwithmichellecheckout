WORKSHOP

Post-Divorce Reset

You survived the divorce. Now it's time to stop surviving and start reclaiming. This workshop is your first real breath of what's possible on the other side.

Book Your Discovery Call

WHO THIS IS FOR

You're past the paperwork. Now what?

Divorce doesn't end when the documents are signed. It ends when you finally stop organizing your life around who you used to be — or who you were in that relationship. This workshop is for the woman who is done grieving and ready to rebuild.

Whether you're 6 months out or 6 years out, if you still feel like you're carrying an old identity that doesn't fit anymore, this is your reset point.

You feel like you lost yourself somewhere in the marriage — or the divorce itself

You're functioning, but not thriving — moving through the motions

You don't know who you are outside of being a wife, a co-parent, or someone's ex

You're ready to stop rebuilding the old life and start architecting a new one

WHAT WE COVER

Four Pillars of the Reset

This workshop moves through the four core areas where divorce leaves the deepest identity marks — and teaches you how to reclaim each one.

Identity Inventory

Audit which parts of your identity were borrowed from the marriage versus which parts are truly and deeply yours.

Pattern Release

Identify the survival responses that served you during the hardest days — and learn how to finally release them.

Nervous System Reset

Learn foundational regulation tools to help your body feel safe enough to want something — and safe enough to go after it.

Forward Architecture

Define who you are becoming — and map the concrete first steps toward living from that identity, starting today.

WHAT YOU WALK AWAY WITH

Clarity you can actually feel.

1

A clear picture of your current identity

You'll leave knowing exactly which beliefs, stories, and roles you've been carrying — and which ones actually belong to you.

2

Regulation tools that work

Practical nervous system techniques you can use in real life — not just in a coaching session — to stay grounded when things get hard.

3

A written identity declaration

A personalized document capturing who you are — not who you were in the marriage — that you can return to whenever the old stories start pulling at you.

4

Your next right step

Whether that's continuing into The Program, booking a private intensive, or simply integrating the reset — you'll leave with clarity about exactly what to do next.
